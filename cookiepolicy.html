Cookie Policy

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience on postwidely.com.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function correctly (e.g., login, security, transactions).

  • Performance Cookies: Collect information about how users interact with our website to help us improve functionality.

  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings (e.g., language, location).

  • Targeting/Advertising Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements based on your interests.

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Recognize returning users and remember preferences;

  • Monitor and analyze site usage for better performance;

  • Deliver tailored content and promotional materials.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. However, some features of the website may not function properly without cookies enabled.

5. Third-Party Cookies

6. Consent

By continuing to use postwidely.com, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy. You can change your cookie preferences at any time in your browser settings.

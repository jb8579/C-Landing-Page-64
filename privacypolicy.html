Privacy Policy

1. Background

The postwidely.com website is owned and operated by Down Under Lotto.
This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you interact with our website. It also explains how you can access or update your data, or raise concerns.
Down Under Lotto is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your personal data is handled in accordance with applicable privacy laws and best practices.

2. Definitions

  • Personal Information means any data that identifies you or could reasonably be used to identify you;

  • Privacy Laws refer to the relevant data protection and privacy regulations applicable to our operations;

  • Website means the official site operated by Down Under Lotto, postwidely.com.

3. What Personal Information do we collect and hold?

We collect Personal Information that is necessary to provide our services to you. Depending on your interaction with us, this may include:

  • Full Name

  • Date of Birth

  • Postal Address

  • Email Address

  • Phone Number

  • Bank Account or Payment Information

  • Credit Card Details

  • Signature

  • Proof of Identity documents as needed

  • Login Credentials (Username/Password)

To protect against fraudulent activity, we may request verification of identity during lottery ticket purchases at our discretion.

4. Why and how we collect Personal Information

We collect your data for the following purposes:

  • Confirm your identity for account setup and security;

  • Enable purchases and manage your lottery subscriptions;

  • Track your ticket history and winnings;

  • Notify winners and process claims;

  • Administer payments, billing, and customer support;

  • Improve and innovate our offerings and website functionality;

  • Send you promotional offers (opt-out available);

  • Prevent fraud and unauthorized access;

  • Respond to your queries or feedback.

Cookies

  • We use cookies to enhance your user experience on our website.

  • Cookies are small data files stored on your browser to help remember your preferences and activity.

  • They enable functionalities such as keeping your cart information or login status intact.

  • Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our services.

For security, identity verification may be required during purchases at our sole discretion.

Your Personal Information is used strictly to provide, manage, and enhance lottery services and to ensure proper delivery of winnings to our Customers.
